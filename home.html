About BrickDuino

At BrickDuino, we are passionate about making electronics, programming, and STEM education accessible and enjoyable for everyone, especially young learners. Our mission is to spark curiosity and inspire creativity through hands-on learning experiences that combine the power of Arduino with the fun and familiarity of Block-based building blocks.

BrickDuino is a modular electronics kit designed specifically for kids and beginners. By integrating programmable Arduino components into easy-to-connect, Block-compatible pieces, we help learners explore the world of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) in a way that feels like play. Whether building simple circuits or creating interactive projects, users of all ages can develop practical skills in coding, electronics, and problem-solving.

We believe that learning should be engaging, intuitive, and fun. That’s why our kits come with step-by-step guides, beginner-friendly tutorials, and creative project ideas to help every learner succeed—regardless of their background or experience level. With BrickDuino, we’re not just teaching electronics, we’re building future innovators, one brick at a time.

Components

This Arduino kit combines the powerful Arduino UNO R3 board with a variety of electronic modules and custom 3D-printed blocks, making it an ideal tool for learning electronics and programming in a fun, hands-on way. Each component is carefully selected to demonstrate different types of input and output functionalities. The use of block-based connections and a block-shaped breadboard ensures a modular, child-friendly, and easy-to-understand setup, making the learning experience interactive and safe.

Through this project, students or beginners will explore real-world applications such as sensing the environment, controlling lights and displays, generating sound, and more—building both logical thinking and technical skills.
